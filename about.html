Blueberry Pi

Blueberry Pi is a tutorial site developed for students of Emily Carr University to learn how to use a single-board computer such as Raspberry Pi to create projects.

All information contents are prepared by faculty of interaction design department

This website is designed and created by Ryan Lan for an interaction design course, INTD-219.
